# First-Time Setup

The first time ELMS starts — whether you just installed the desktop app or your firm's cloud instance has just been deployed — a setup wizard walks you through creating your firm profile and administrator account. This only runs once.

> [!NOTE]
> The setup process is only available before any administrator account exists. Once setup is complete, this wizard is permanently disabled and the setup page cannot be accessed again. If you need to re-run setup, contact your system administrator or ELMS support.

---

## Step 1 — Language Selection

The first screen lets you choose the display language for ELMS.

1. Select your preferred language:
   - **العربية (Arabic)** — Right-to-left layout; the navigation sidebar appears on the right side of the screen. This is the default.
   - **English** — Left-to-right layout.
   - **Français (French)** — Left-to-right layout.
2. Click **Next** (or the equivalent button in your chosen language).

> [!NOTE]
> You can change the language later from your profile settings. Individual team members can each choose their own preferred language independently.

---

## Step 2 — Firm Name and Type

Enter basic information about your firm.

1. **Firm Name**: Enter the full official name of your law firm as it should appear on invoices, client communications, and reports.
2. **Firm Type**: Select the option that best describes your firm:
   - **Solo** — A single practitioner
   - **Small Firm** — 2 to 10 lawyers
   - **Medium Firm** — 11 to 50 lawyers
   - **Enterprise** — More than 50 lawyers
3. Click **Next**.

---

## Step 3 — Create the Administrator Account

This step creates the first account in ELMS. This account will have full administrator access to all features and settings.

1. **Full Name**: Enter the administrator's full name.
2. **Email Address**: Enter the email address that will be used to log in. This address will also receive system notifications.
3. **Password**: Choose a strong password (at least 8 characters; a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols is recommended).
4. **Confirm Password**: Re-enter the same password to confirm it.
5. Click **Next**.

> [!NOTE]
> Keep the administrator credentials in a safe place. If you lose access to the administrator account, recovering it requires direct access to the database. There is no self-service password reset for the administrator at this stage.

---

## Step 4 — Firm Settings

Configure the core settings for your firm.

1. **Timezone**: Select your local timezone from the dropdown. For most Egyptian firms, this is **Africa/Cairo (UTC+2)** or **UTC+3** during daylight saving time. This setting affects how hearing dates, deadlines, and notification times are calculated.
2. **Default Currency**: Select the currency used for invoices. The default is **Egyptian Pound (EGP)**.
3. Click **Next**.

---

## Step 5 — Completion

ELMS confirms that setup is complete.

1. A summary screen displays the firm name and administrator email you entered.
2. Click **Go to Dashboard** (or the equivalent button).
3. The ELMS dashboard loads — you are now logged in as the firm administrator.

---

## After Setup — Recommended Next Steps

Now that your firm is set up, here are the most important things to do next:

1. **Review your user directory**: Open **Users** to confirm the administrator account and any existing team records.
2. **Configure notification preferences**: Open **Settings → Notifications** to choose the channels visible in the current frontend UI.
3. **Add your first client**: See [Managing Clients](../core-workflows/05-managing-clients.md).
4. **Open your first case**: See [Managing Cases](../core-workflows/06-managing-cases.md).

> [!NOTE]
> Invite-based team onboarding is not exposed in the current frontend UI, so this setup guide focuses on the screens that are currently available after first launch.
