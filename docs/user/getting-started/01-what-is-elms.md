# What Is ELMS?

ELMS (Electronic Legal Management System) is a practice management platform designed specifically for law firms in Egypt and the MENA region. It brings everything your firm needs — clients, cases, court hearings, documents, billing, and legal research — into one organized, secure system.

Instead of tracking cases in spreadsheets, storing documents in scattered folders, and chasing payments by hand, ELMS gives your entire team a single place to work together, whether you are in the office or working remotely.

---

## Who Is ELMS For?

ELMS is built for everyone in a law firm:

| Role | What they do in ELMS |
|---|---|
| **Firm Administrator** | Manages the firm's settings, team members, billing, and has full access to all features |
| **Senior Lawyer** | Handles cases, clients, assignments, and invoicing |
| **Junior Lawyer** | Works on assigned cases, tasks, and documents |
| **Paralegal** | Manages tasks, uploads documents, and supports case work |
| **Secretary** | Schedules hearings, performs lookups, and handles basic document management |

---

## The Two Editions

ELMS comes in two editions. Both offer the same full feature set — the difference is how and where the software runs.

### Desktop App

The desktop app is installed directly on your computer (Windows, macOS, or Linux). It runs entirely offline and stores all data locally on your machine.

**Choose the Desktop App if:**
- Your firm needs to work without a reliable internet connection
- You prefer to keep all data on your own hardware
- You are a solo practitioner or a small firm without IT infrastructure

> [!NOTE]
> The desktop app bundles its own database. No separate server or database software is required.

### Cloud / Web App

The cloud edition is hosted for your firm and accessed through any modern web browser. Your data lives on a secure server, and your team can log in from anywhere.

**Choose the Cloud App if:**
- Your team works across multiple locations or devices
- You want automatic updates without manual installation
- You prefer a managed, always-available service

## Languages Supported

ELMS supports three languages:

- **Arabic** (default) — full right-to-left (RTL) layout; the navigation sidebar appears on the right side of the screen
- **English** — standard left-to-right layout
- **French** — standard left-to-right layout

You can switch the display language at any time from your profile settings.

## Feature Overview

| Feature | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Client Management** | Store and organize client records — individuals, companies, and government entities |
| **Case Management** | Track case numbers, court stages, status, parties, and the full lifecycle of each matter |
| **Court Hearings** | Schedule sessions, record outcomes, and receive automatic reminders |
| **Tasks & Deadlines** | Assign tasks to team members with priorities and due dates |
| **Document Management** | Upload files in any common format; ELMS extracts text automatically for full-text search |
| **Billing & Invoicing** | Create invoices, record payments, track expenses, and generate financial reports |
| **Law Library** | Browse Egyptian legislation, court rulings, and scholarly annotations |
| **AI Research Assistant** | Ask legal questions and receive AI-generated answers with cited sources |
| **Client Portal** | Allow clients to log in and view the status of their own cases |
| **Team Management** | Invite team members, assign roles, and control who can access what |
| **Notifications** | Receive reminders by in-app alert, email, SMS, or desktop notification |
